Creative vision. Solid experience. Powerhouse team.

Delta Holdings &Development, LLC (“DHD”) specializes in creating master-planned communities that include world-class Hotel and Casino Resorts, residential neighborhoods, retail and restaurants, commercial, medical, solar parks, airports, golf courses, and entertainment complexes. Our respective development partners, operating management team and design associates are leaders in their respective fields.

DHD recently expanded its growth and are now a participating partner and majority/managing partner in two large-scale joint ventures, both U.S. and Internationally.

Riverbend Crossing (“Riverbend”) is a joint venture with Phillips Development and includes a 4,710-acre master planned resort community project in DeSoto County, Mississippi. Located along the Mississippi River and bordering the Gaming District in Tunica, Mississippi, the project is a mixed-use development including residential, commercial, entertainment and recreational elements, all surrounding a project-wide 600 acre man-made lake system. The project is highlighted by a Paramount Pictures, Inc. movie-based themed retail and entertainment center with branding, retail and intellectual properties. Once completed, Riverbend will contain the Paramount Entertainment Center, resort hotels, hotel/conference centers, shopping areas, golf courses, active adult villages and residential housing.

The project site is accessed by two major interstate highways and is located within the Memphis, Tennessee Metropolitan Statistical Area (the “Memphis MSA”). The Memphis MSA is home to over 1.3 million people. Riverbend is structured to capitalize on the growth occurring in and around the area, and its elements are designed to attract residents and tourists alike. In addition, Riverbend will complement the adjacent gaming venues in Tunica, Mississippi, which receive over 11 million visitors annually, and will provide unique access to these attractions while still providing non-gaming entertainment options. This development will create over 50,000 jobs and is the largest master-planned community in the State of Mississippi.

SerenFusion Americas is a joint venture with Sun Fund Americas to design and develop master-planned resort communities in 13 South and Central American countries, including Costa Rica, Belize, Panama, Mexico, Colombia, Argentina, Chile, Puerto Rico, Uruguay, Brazil, Dominican Republic, Ecuador, and Cuba. The first phase of each community will include the development and installation of 500 - 1,000 acre solar parks with +50 MW utility scale photovoltaic (“PV”) power projects in strategic renewable energy locations. Our solar parks will improve the national and local communities through significant renewable energy infrastructure investments and by creating valuable local clean-tech jobs that will provide long-term local economic stability.

Teaming Partners.

Master Planning, Design, Branding

Delta Holdings and Development, LLC (“DHD”) has teamed up with Leo A. Daly (“LAD”) for master planning, design, and branding of their domestic and international developments.

LAD is an internationally recognized, award-winning architecture, engineering, planning, interior design, and program management firm. It is consistently ranked among the top design firms in the world. Since 1915, it has had an unyielding focus on design excellence to create exceptional spaces that enhance and enrich the human experience. With over 800 professionals in more than 30 office worldwide, its diverse portfolio includes projects in a wide range of markets in more than 70 countries, all 50 US states, and the District of Columbia.

LAD’S gaming experience encompasses the full spectrum of casino design, spanning mega-resorts of Las Vegas to Native American casinos to themed entertainment venues worldwide. Hospitality clients include recognized industry giants such as owners/operators/franchisees as Bristol Hotels, ClubCorp of America, Coast Resorts, Doubletree, Hilton, Holiday Inn, Hyatt InterContinental, Marriott, Sheraton, and Westin to name just a few. While working internationally since 1963. LAD has completed over 400 projects in over 65 countries, including 13 foreign embassies.

DHD’s principles and LAD’s senior management have a long-term working relationship and have designed and developed numerous casino and resort properties for well over twenty years, including numerous Trump Casino Properties in Atlantic City, New Jersey and throughout the Gulf Coast and Riverfront projects in Mississippi.

Golf Courses, Sports Facilities, &Training Academies

DHD’s communities include golf courses designed by Jack Nicklaus as well as his Signature Academies, delivering an unparalleled range of facility programming and training curriculums for all ages.

As the name implies, a Jack Nicklaus Signature Golf Course truly bears the signature of the world’s preeminent golf course designer. It has the thumbprint of arguably the greatest mind in golf history. It is the realization of one man’s vision, philosophy and passion for the greatest game of all. As Jack Nicklaus often says, what ends up on the top of a piece of ground is a piece of him. But, as the Golden Bear also adds, what goes beneath that piece of ground is a collaboration of a talented team that includes designers who work both in the field and in the design studio. It is a team of designers trained in landscape architecture, agronomy and the most state-of-the-art technology the industry has to offer. Nicklaus Design takes pride in the team it has assembled worldwide in offices that span both coasts of the United States, as well as Asia, Europe, Mexico and South Africa. Perhaps one of Jack’s greatest sources of pride is the group of Design Associates who make up the backbone of the world’s leading course design firm.

At Signature Academies, they believe life was not meant to be lived on the sidelines. They believe sports play an essential role in living well, living full. In family togetherness. In health, longevity, fitness, confidence and teamwork. For young and old alike, the lessons learned in sports carry through to every aspect of a well-rounded life. With this in mind, Signature Academies has assembled some of the greatest names and brands in sports – winners of Majors, World Cups, Grand Slams and Olympic medals – to develop a collection of state-of-the-art sports facilities and curriculum that seamlessly integrate into any community or resort.

Imagine a development with open space paths that lead to an oversized golf training center, an Olympic-sized aquatic center, premier soccer pitches, an Openquality tennis complex, and a comprehensive fitness facility – all featuring the most innovative training programs and learning aids in the world, combined with a curriculum designed to support participants of every age, ability and ambition. Welcome to the exclusive LifeSport™ Community by Signature Academies.

Signature academies communities are for developers who understand the value of differentiation. Signature Academies, through its unique concept and world class brands, provides real-world value and momentum to innovative developers, by attracting active, successful and motivated prospects who seek a full, rich lifestyle within a truly distinctive community.

Colin Hegarty, president of Golf Research Group studies the impact of golf course architects on property values and says that the best value-added brand is Jack Nicklaus. Since 2000, Nicklaus has designed 75 residential courses with an average net value of the overall project (golf and real estate) of $245.9 million.

"Architects value numbers are relevant when you're thinking of buying a home at one community versus another," says Hegarty. "Homes at Nicklaus communities are like blue chip stocks - their value grows dependably every year."
